What is Oasis?

Oasis Ascent is a short-term, stabilization treatment center for teens that are struggling with mental health. We see both males and females ages 12-17 for up to 3 months for intensive support in their mental health journey. We focus on DBT skills as our main therapeutic practice but the students also participate in individual therapy, group therapy, family therapy, recreation therapy, and more.
